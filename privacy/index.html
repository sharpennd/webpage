Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot verification service that integrates with Roblox.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our verification service, we collect:

  • Discord Information: Your Discord user ID, username, and server membership
  • Roblox Information: Your Roblox user ID, username, and display name obtained through Roblox OAuth
  • Group Information: Your Roblox group membership and rank (if applicable)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Verify your Roblox account and link it to your Discord account
  • Assign appropriate Discord roles based on your Roblox group rank
  • Update your Discord nickname to match your Roblox username
  • Manage server access and permissions

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely and we implement appropriate technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. We only retain your data as long as necessary to provide our services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others
  • With your explicit consent

6. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Discord: Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
  • Roblox: Subject to Roblox's Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your verification data at any time by contacting server administrators. Upon request, we will remove your stored verification information within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for users who meet Discord's and Roblox's minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect information from users under 13 years of age without parental consent as required by applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us via Discord or email.
